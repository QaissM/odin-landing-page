Learn Web development the right way!

The Odin Project (TOP) is a free, open‑source online curriculum that teaches full‑stack web development. It focuses heavily on hands‑on, project‑based learning, guiding learners through HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, Node.js, and more. Instead of passive tutorials, TOP pushes you to build real projects, collaborate with others, and learn by doing—similar to how developers work in the real world.
